## Scoring guide

| Score | Meaning |
|---|---|
| 5 | Exceeds requirements; automated validation and clear ownership |
| 4 | Meets requirements with minor, tracked gaps |
| 3 | Meets most requirements; some controls are manual or untested |
| 2 | Significant gaps; missing owner, validation, or documentation |
| 1 | Critical gaps; high residual risk |
| 0 | Not started or unknown |
