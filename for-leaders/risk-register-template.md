## Legend

- **Likelihood**: Rare / Unlikely / Possible / Likely / Almost certain
- **Impact**: Negligible / Low / Medium / High / Severe
- **Status**: Open / In progress / Mitigated / Accepted / Closed

## Tips

- Review the register monthly or before each major model release.
- Link each risk to a control in the security assessment checklist.
- Close a risk only when the mitigation is tested and validated.
